About Us

Scotland's dedicated heat pump installation specialists

Our Story

Heat Pump Installation Scotland was founded with a simple mission: to help Scottish homeowners and businesses make the switch to clean, efficient, renewable heating.

With years of experience in the heating industry and a passion for sustainable energy, we specialise exclusively in heat pump technology. This focus means we deliver expert advice, quality installations, and reliable aftercare — every time.

Based in Scotland, we understand the unique challenges of heating homes in our climate. From exposed Highland properties to city centre flats, we design systems that work in real Scottish conditions.

We also work as a trusted subcontractor for other heating companies, builders, and property developers. Whether it's a one-off project or an ongoing partnership, our MCS-certified team delivers the same high standard of work under your brand as we do under ours.
