Comprehensive heat pump solutions for Scottish homes, businesses, and trade partners

🌡️ Air Source Heat Pump Installation

Air source heat pumps extract heat from the outside air to warm your home and hot water — even when temperatures drop below freezing. They're the most popular choice for Scottish homeowners switching to renewable heating.

What's included:

  • Free home assessment and site survey
  • System design tailored to your property
  • Full installation by MCS-certified engineers
  • Integration with existing radiators or underfloor heating
  • Commissioning and handover
  • Manufacturer warranty support
Request a Free Survey

🌍 Ground Source Heat Pump Installation

Ground source heat pumps use pipes buried underground to extract stable heat from the earth. They offer the highest efficiency of any heat pump type and are ideal for larger properties or new builds.

What's included:

  • Geological assessment and ground survey
  • Borehole or trench installation
  • Full system design and installation
  • Integration with your heating system
  • Commissioning and performance testing
  • Ongoing support and warranty
Request a Free Survey
